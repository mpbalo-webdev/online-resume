Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy reading nonfiction books focused on self-development and psychology, particularly topics related to human behavior, mindset, and personal growth. Gardening herbs and spices is a relaxing hobby that helps me practice patience, consistency, and attention to detail. I also enjoy cooking, especially traditional Visayan dishes such as cansi, as well as simple desserts like mango float, which allows me to explore creativity and culture through food.

Journaling is part of my routine for organizing thoughts, reflecting on life experiences, and maintaining mental clarity. In my downtime, I listen to personal development and leadership podcasts, including shows by Lewis Howes and Mel Robbins, to stay motivated and continuously improve my mindset and habits.
